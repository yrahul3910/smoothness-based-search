Smoothness-Guided Optimization — Opus Experiment Rounds

Extending SMOOTHIE (Yedida & Menzies, 2025) to gradient-free settings on SE datasets. Each round below targets a different task and dataset family; the metric differs accordingly.

See also Round 1 (Exps 1–5: establishing β-smoothness for trees and the INGS surrogate).

Round 2 — Process datasets (regression / surrogate-HPO)

data/optimize/process/ metric: R² 10 datasets

Task: tune a decision tree's hyperparameters to best predict each dataset's aggregate effort/cost objective; score by test R². COC1000, NASA93, POM3a–d, XOMO flight/ground/osp/osp2.

ExpQuestion
6 — Fair comparisonHow much of TPE/BOHB's reported win was test-set peeking?
7 — SMASDoes combining val-R² and β reach parity with real BOHB using only training data?
8 — SMAS-ScreenDoes SMOOTHIE's cheap two-stage budget transfer to trees?

Bottom line: SMAS reaches parity with real BOHB (training-data only); TPE stays the quality leader; the cheap-screen trick gives only a modest ~1.6× speedup for trees.

Round 3 — Configuration datasets (direct optimization)

data/optimize/config/ metric: distance-to-heaven 8 datasets

Task: search each software system's configuration space directly (pool-based active optimization over a lookup table); minimize distance-to-heaven of the best config found within an evaluation budget. SS-A/B/I/O, Apache, X264, SQL, HSMGP.
